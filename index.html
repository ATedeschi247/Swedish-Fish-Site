About

Swedish candy company Malaco originally created the product exclusively in Sweden. They only desired to expand into America in the late 1950s and entered into a partnership with Cadbury to do so. They specifically did this with their fish candy product in order to reflect Swedish culture, which is heavily dependent on fish.
